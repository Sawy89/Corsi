Sorin group - Biomedical Engineer

My first job experience after my degree started as a trainee in 2014 in a big biomedical multinational, on the Business Unit where pace-makers and ICD (implantable cardiac defribillator) were made. I was lucky: the job was very close to my thesis experience, to my studies, I loved it! I had to write clinical tests on new devices, recover and perform data analysis on signal from clinical studies, give clinical support to other department on the same Business Unit, ... I liked it very much: we were trying to improve heart performances by changing pacing sites and timings! Also the team was good: some italian in a french group, I had the opportunity to know smart people and important physicians, to travel a bit in Europe (now I know very well Paris and a little of Strasbourg and Maastricht), and to improve my english, my french (a little) and my idea of the world. My experience there went on for 2 years, after which a global re-organization of the company structure made me look around...
